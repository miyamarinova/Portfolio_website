The Chat Rooms

Tha Chat Rooms are Flask web application that brings together real-time chat functionality, a mental health assistant powered by the ChatGBT API, and an anonymous story-sharing feature in a blog-style format.

Key Features:

  • Real-Time Chat:Experience seamless communication with real-time chat using the Socket library. Engage in conversations, connect with others, and enjoy the dynamic interaction within the application.
  • Mental Health Assistant:Integrated with the ChatGBT API, the web app includes a mental health assistant designed to provide support and assistance. Users can engage in conversations, ask questions, and receive empathetic responses to enhance their mental well-being.
  • Anonymous Story Sharing:Share your stories in a safe and anonymous environment. Whether it's personal experiences, thoughts, or reflections, the blog-style feature allows users to express themselves without revealing their identity.

Technologies Used:

  • Flask: The web app is built on the Flask framework, providing a lightweight and modular foundation for the project.
  • Bootstrap: Frontend Styling
  • SQLAlchemy: Database for the shared stories.
  • Socket Library: Real-time chat functionality is achieved through the integration of the Socket library, enabling instant communication between users.
  • ChatGBT API: The mental health assistant leverages the ChatGBT API, offering a unique and supportive conversational experience for users seeking mental health resources.
